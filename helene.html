Hurricane Helene Spatial Analysis

This project models precipitation during Hurricane Helene using spatial regression and kriging techniques. The goal was to capture spatial dependence and improve prediction accuracy across affected regions.

Methods

  • Spatial regression
  • Variogram modeling
  • Kriging interpolation

Key Insight

The model identified strong spatial clustering in precipitation patterns, improving predictive performance over non-spatial methods.
